Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you go to?

All over Palm Beach County. If you have no room to do your car, you can come to our spot to get your car done at a 20% discount.

How long does it take to detail my car?

Depending on the size and condition of the vehicle. For our basic interior and exterior (Essential Clean Package), a sedan would take on average 1.5 hrs.

Is there a travel fee?

Nope, it is all built into the price.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash, card, and Zelle. There is a 3.5% fee on card payments.

Will you come to my place of work?

Yes, we have been to many job locations!

Who is coming to detail my car?

Your car will be detailed by 2-3 detailers, but for sure it will be detailed by Wayne and Wayne Jr.!

What is the difference between a ceramic coating and wax?

A ceramic coating offers long protection of your car's paint after one application. Were talking years of protection. Wax is more temporary, but a consistent wash and wax is very beneficial for your car's paint — future washes will be easier, and it will be less scratch prone. Chances of fading will decrease as well.
